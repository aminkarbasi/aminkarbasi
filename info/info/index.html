 Streaming Submodular Maximization under a k-Set System Constraint.

Welcome to the I.I.D. group, led by Amin Karbasi. The research in our group is at the intersection of learning theory[1,2], optimization[3,4], and information processing[5,6]. We develop data-driven algorithms, with strong theoretical guarantees, that can automatically acquire and reason about highly uncertain information. For more information, please visit our research directions.
